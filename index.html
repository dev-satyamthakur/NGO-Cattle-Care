About Us

Mission and Vision

At the Sri Radha Krishna Mandir Gaushala Trust, our mission is to provide a safe and loving haven for abandoned and distressed cows, while fostering a harmonious coexistence between these gentle beings and our communities. We are committed to ensuring the welfare, protection, and preservation of cows, which are an integral part of our cultural heritage and a source of numerous blessings.

Our vision is to create a world where every cow finds a caring and protective shelter, where our communities thrive in harmony with these sacred animals, and where the values of compassion, sustainability, and respect for life are upheld. We aim to inspire individuals and communities to embrace the significance of cows in our culture, understanding that by caring for them, we enrich our own lives and contribute to a healthier, more compassionate world.
